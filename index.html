About Bugatti

Bugatti is a French luxury automobile manufacturer known for its high-performance sports cars and exclusive luxury vehicles. Founded in 1909 by Ettore Bugatti, the brand has a rich history of innovation and excellence.

Performance and Engineering

Bugatti cars are renowned for their performance, featuring powerful engines and advanced aerodynamics. The Chiron, for example, boasts an 8.0-liter quad-turbocharged W16 engine, producing 1,479 horsepower and capable of reaching speeds of over 300 mph
